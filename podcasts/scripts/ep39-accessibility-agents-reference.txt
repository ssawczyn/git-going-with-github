For enforcement, you use Hooks. Hooks are JSON files that execute shell commands at specific lifecycle events. Think of them like a building inspector who comes in at specific phases of construction to check your work.

[JAMIE]
Oh, I like that. So the inspector stops the work, checks the code, and either approves it or blocks it.
